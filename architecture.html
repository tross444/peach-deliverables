Four Plays · Two Lanes
REV 1.0

Instead of blasting a static list, we map the entire market and only reach out when companies show they're in-market. Four input sources, two delivery lanes. High-volume segments (fintech lenders, banks and credit unions) flow through an automated outreach pipeline. Salesforce CRM accounts route to Hugo for high-touch outreach where he already has relationship context. Website visitors get automated outreach while the intent is fresh.

Lane Type A
Automated Outreach
Signal fires. The system finds verified contacts, writes personalized outreach, and sends it. Zero manual steps.
Lane Type B
Hugo Alert Lane
Signal fires. Slack alert to Hugo with company context and key contacts. Hugo handles outreach his way.
