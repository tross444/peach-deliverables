What Travis built

The short version

Travis built an automated system that monitors 646 fintech lending companies every day for signs they might need what Peach offers. When the system spots something, like a company hiring lending engineers, raising money, or launching a new product, it automatically finds the right people to contact, writes personalized emails that reference the specific event, verifies every email address, and loads everything into SmartLead for Hugo to review.

No manual research. No generic templates. Every outreach is triggered by a real event and personalized to it.

Suggested framing

"Travis built a system that watches our entire TAM for buying signals. When something happens at a company, like a key hire or a product launch, the system catches it and generates personalized outreach automatically. Hugo reviews and sends. It ran a live test today. I'll show you the results."

What it replaces

The before and after

Right now, outbound is manual. Someone has to research companies, decide who to target, figure out the angle, write the email, find the contact info. That takes hours per company and the timing is mostly luck. You might reach out the week before something happens, or six months after.

This system flips that. Instead of guessing who to contact and when, it watches for the moment a company starts showing buying signals and reaches out while the signal is fresh. The research, the personalization, the contact finding, all of that happens automatically.

Suggested framing

"The biggest problem with our current outbound isn't the copy or the list. It's timing. We're reaching out to companies based on when we get around to it, not based on when they're actually in-market. This system solves the timing problem."

What changes for Hugo

His expertise matters more, not less

Hugo stops doing research and starts doing sales. Instead of spending time figuring out who to call and why, he opens SmartLead and sees contacts who are already there because something real happened at their company. The email is written. The signal is referenced. His job is to review the outreach, apply his judgment about the relationship, and decide when to engage.

The system handles the groundwork so Hugo can focus on the conversations that actually close deals. Nothing sends without his review.

Suggested framing for Hugo

"This isn't about replacing what you do. It's about giving you better ammunition. Right now you're spending time on research that could be automated. This gives you that time back and makes sure you're reaching out at the exact right moment, with a reason to call."

Suggested framing for Jamie

"The system ran a live test today. 8 companies, 14 real signals detected, 10 verified contacts, 40 personalized emails. Every factual claim in every email traced back to source data. Zero fabricated. 180 automated tests. Travis can walk you through the architecture if you want to see how the pipeline works."

Jamie is an engineer. He'll want to know how it works, not just that it works. The proof doc (tross444.github.io/peach-deliverables, pw: peach2026) has a full pipeline breakdown he can explore. Point him there if he wants the technical detail.
